# SOC 2 Compliance Training

**Course ID:** soc2-compliance  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Duration:** ~45 minutes  
**Passing Score:** 80%

## Overview

This course teaches employees the fundamentals of SOC 2 compliance and their role in maintaining it. Designed for SaaS organizations pursuing or maintaining SOC 2 certification.

## Modules

1. **Introduction to SOC 2** (4 slides)
   - What is SOC 2
   - Type I vs Type II
   - Business importance

2. **Trust Services Criteria** (8 slides)
   - Security (Common Criteria)
   - Availability
   - Processing Integrity
   - Confidentiality
   - Privacy

3. **Your Security Responsibilities** (6 slides)
   - Password & authentication security
   - Access control & least privilege
   - Data handling best practices

4. **Recognizing & Reporting Threats** (5 slides)
   - Common security threats
   - Phishing & social engineering
   - Incident reporting procedures

5. **Staying Compliant** (5 slides)
   - Change management
   - Documentation & audit trails
   - Vendor & third-party risk
