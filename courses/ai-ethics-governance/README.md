# AI Ethics & Governance Training

**Course ID:** ai-ethics-governance  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Duration:** ~40 minutes  
**Passing Score:** 80%

## Overview

This course teaches employees the fundamentals of AI ethics, governance frameworks, and compliance requirements. Covers the EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, bias management, and responsible AI practices.

## Modules

1. **Introduction to AI Ethics** (4 slides)
   - What is AI ethics
   - Core principles
   - AI governance overview

2. **AI Regulations & Frameworks** (6 slides)
   - Global regulatory landscape
   - EU AI Act risk categories
   - NIST AI Risk Management Framework
   - General-Purpose AI rules

3. **AI Risk Management** (5 slides)
   - Types of AI risks
   - Risk assessment process
   - Human oversight requirements

4. **Bias, Fairness & Transparency** (6 slides)
   - Understanding AI bias
   - Real-world bias examples
   - Transparency requirements
   - Bias testing & mitigation

5. **Responsible AI in Practice** (5 slides)
   - Your role in AI governance
   - Using AI tools responsibly
   - Documentation requirements
