Frequently Asked Questions

How does licensing work?

You purchase an annual license per course (or bundle). This gives you unlimited use for all employees in your organization for one year.

What format are the courses?

All courses are delivered as SCORM 1.2 packages. They work with any SCORM-compliant LMS including Moodle, Cornerstone, Workday Learning, and more.

Can I try before I buy?

Yes! Contact us for a demo or a 14-day trial of any course.

What languages are supported?

All courses include English, Spanish, and French. Additional languages available on request.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes. For organizations with 500+ employees or custom needs, contact us for enterprise pricing.
